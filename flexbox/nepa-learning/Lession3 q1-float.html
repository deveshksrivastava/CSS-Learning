Use of float

    >float: none; The element does not float. This is the default value..
    >float: left; The element floats to the left of its container. Content that follows will flow around the right side of the element.
    >float: right; The element floats to the right of its container. Content that follows will flow around the left side of the element.!
    >float: inherit; The element inherits the float value from its parent element
